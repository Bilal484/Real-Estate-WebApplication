About Us

A wholesaler is a business entity that purchases goods in bulk from manufacturers or producers and resells them to retailers, other businesses, or institutions. They do not typically sell directly to consumers. Wholesalers allow manufacturers to focus on production while helping retailers access a wide range of products efficiently.
